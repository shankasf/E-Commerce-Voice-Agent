## Common Interview Process at Docusign
Based on the scraped data, the software role interview process typically follows these steps:
1.  **Recruiter Screen**: Initial call covering background, visa status, and interest.
2.  **Technical Assessment**: Coding challenges (LeetCode style), take-home assignments, or technical screenings.
3.  **Hiring Manager Interview**: Deep dive into experience using the STAR method.
4.  **Panel Interview / Deep Dive**: Live coding, system design, and peer reviews.
5.  **Presentation**: For some roles (Solutions Engineer, Senior roles), a product demo or case study presentation is required.
6.  **Final Review**: Strategic and cultural fit assessment with a Director or Executive.
