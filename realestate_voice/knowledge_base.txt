## Lease Policies

### Rent Payment
- **Due Date**: 1st of each month
- **Grace Period**: Until the 5th (no late fee)
- **Late Fee**: $50 on the 6th, plus $25/day after
- **Payment Methods**: Online portal, check, money order, cash at office
- **Auto-Pay**: Available through tenant portal

### Security Deposit
- Equal to one month's rent
- Refundable within 30 days of move-out
- Deductions for damages beyond normal wear

### Lease Terms
- **Standard**: 12-month lease
- **Month-to-Month**: Available at 10-15% premium
- **Short-Term (6 months)**: Available at 5% premium
- **Renewal Notice**: 60 days before expiration

### Move-Out Requirements
- Written 60-day notice required
- Move-out inspection scheduled
- Unit must be clean and in good condition
- All keys returned to office
- Forwarding address provided

## Pet Policy

## FAQ

**Q: How do I pay rent?**
A: Through the online tenant portal, by mail, or at the office during business hours.

**Q: How do I report a maintenance issue?**
A: Call the office, use the tenant portal, or for emergencies, call 555-911-0000.

**Q: Can I have a pet?**
A: Yes, with restrictions. Maximum 2 pets, weight limits apply, requires pet deposit and monthly pet rent.

**Q: When can I renew my lease?**
A: Contact us 60-90 days before your lease ends to discuss renewal options.

**Q: What utilities am I responsible for?**
A: Typically electric and gas. Water/sewer/trash may be included - check your lease.

**Q: How do I add someone to my lease?**
A: Contact the leasing office. Additional occupants require application and approval.

**Q: Is renter's insurance required?**
A: Yes, minimum $100,000 liability coverage is required.
